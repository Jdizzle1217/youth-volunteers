Who We Are

We're a nonprofit volunteer service network that helps teenagers aged 13+ gain meaningful community service experience while supporting busy families with everyday tasks. All work is voluntary and unpaid, with verified hours for school, scholarships, and college applications.

Our Mission

To empower teenagers to build responsibility, confidence, and real-world experience through meaningful community service, while providing support to families in our community. We connect motivated teen volunteers with safe, age-appropriate opportunities and provide official documentation of their verified service hours.

Hour Verification

We provide verified documentation that is accepted by most schools as independent community service. Our verification process includes:

  • Adult oversight and program supervision
  • Digital hour tracking with family confirmation
  • Official verification letters signed by our program advisor
  • Detailed service records including dates, tasks, and supervisors
  • Independent verification available to schools upon request

All service is unpaid, community-based, and designed to meet standard requirements for school, club, and scholarship applications.

Program Oversight

Our program operates under adult supervision with clear safety protocols and accountability measures. An adult advisor oversees all matches and can be contacted by schools or families to verify service hours and program legitimacy.
